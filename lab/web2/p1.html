Kudla Beach, Karnataka

it is located near Mangalore in Karnataka, is a coastal destination known for its golden sands, gentle waves, and peaceful sunsets.

Experiences

Visitors can enjoy peaceful walks, beachside activities, and fresh seafood prepared by local vendors.

Highlight

Sunset views, calm waters, and the local Mangalorean delicacies make KudlaBeach special.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: October to March.
  • Carry light clothing and sunscreen.
  • Try local seafood for an authentic experience.
