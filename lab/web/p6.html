About robotics

Robotics is an interdisciplinary field of science and engineering focused on the design, construction, and use of robots that can perform tasks, assist humans, or substitute for human actions.

Applications of robotics

Robotics has expanded far beyond its traditional roots in manufacturing into a diverse array of applications, transforming numerous industries by automating tasks that are dirty, dangerous, dull, or require high precision.
