Jog Falls, Karnataka

Jog Falls, located in the Shivamogga district of Karnataka, is one of the highest waterfalls in India. Known for its majestic vertical drop, it offers a breathtaking view especially during the monsoon season.

Experiences

Tourists can enjoy panoramic viewpoints, trekking paths around the falls, and the thundering roar of cascading water.

Highlight

The four distinct cascades—Raja, Rani, Rover, and Rocket—make Jog Falls a unique natural wonder.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: June to September.
  • Wear comfortable shoes.
  • Carry rain protection.
